--Mushroom Soup--

--Ingredients--
230g mushrooms
2tbsp onions
2 garlic cloves
2tbsp butter
3 tbsp flour
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup evaporated milk
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp nutmeg

--Method--
Cut the mushrooms into slices.
Melt butter in large frying pan.
Add in onions, garlic, and mushrooms. 
Cook until onions are soft.

Blend in 2 tbsps. flour and stir.
Add in the chicken broth and heat until slightly thickened while stirring frequently.
Stir cream with additional 1 tbsp. flour and seasonings. Add in cream to soup. Heat to thicken while stirring frequently.
Serve and enjoy!
